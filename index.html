About Us

We are a weekly newsletter and blog for foodies.

Hi There! We are The Foodie Maps

Our mission is to bring foodies and local restaurants closer together. We hope to keep passionate foodies informed about their nearby food scene and tell the stories of local businesses. We also support local influencers by featuring them in our newsletters.

Each weekly post follows a theme, like pizza! We then find and visit local restaurants and interview their owners to learn more about their story and their food. Then, we share all of our experiences with you!

Our social media presence has created a community of foodies online. We are actively recruiting guest writers and foodies looking to grow their following. Let us know if that sounds like you!
